## 2024-05-24 - Modal Dialog Accessibility
**Learning:** Custom modal components must have proper WAI-ARIA roles (`dialog`) and attributes (`aria-modal`, `aria-labelledby`, `aria-describedby`) to be effectively announced by screen readers, making them accessible to users with visual impairments.
**Action:** Always verify custom modal/dialog components include complete ARIA definitions and keyboard trap/focus management to ensure robust accessibility.
